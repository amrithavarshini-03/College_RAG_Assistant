College Scholarship and Financial Aid Policy

1. Merit Scholarship

Students who achieve a CGPA of 9.0 or higher in the previous semester are eligible for the Merit Scholarship.

Benefits:
- 50% tuition fee waiver
- Certificate of academic excellence

2. Need-Based Financial Aid

Students from economically weaker backgrounds may apply for financial assistance.

Eligibility Requirements:
- Family income below ₹3,00,000 per year
- Submission of income certificate
- Recommendation from department head

3. Government Scholarships

Students may apply for government-funded scholarships such as:

- National Scholarship Scheme
- Minority Scholarship
- SC/ST Financial Assistance Program

4. Application Process

Steps to apply:
1. Fill the scholarship application form on the student portal.
2. Upload required documents (income certificate, mark sheets).
3. Submit before the deadline announced each semester.

5. Deadline

Scholarship applications must be submitted within the first 30 days of the semester.
